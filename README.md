# 📘 Consulting Casebook 2026

A comprehensive consulting casebook developed for **FinCom, IIT Ropar** to help students prepare for consulting interviews, case competitions, and strategy roles.

This project consolidates industry-standard consulting frameworks, structured problem-solving approaches, business case studies, and interview resources into a single reference guide for aspiring consultants.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project was to create a practical and structured learning resource covering the core concepts required in consulting recruitment.

The casebook focuses on:

- Case Interview Preparation
- Business Strategy
- Market Entry
- Profitability
- Growth Strategy
- Pricing Strategy
- Guesstimates
- Consulting Frameworks
- Business Problem Solving
